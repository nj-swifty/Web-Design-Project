Jalapeno Purple

A purple fruiting variant of the popular Mexican chilli. Purple Jalapeños fruit prolifically and are quite easy to grow. They will do o outside in a warm, sheltered spot. Fruits are mild to medium heat, around 5,000 SHU. The flavour is akin to the green version of the Jalapeño, but a little sweeter.
