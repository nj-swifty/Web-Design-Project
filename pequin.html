Pequin

An extremely hot little bird type chilli that produces fiery little blunt pods. Pods can reach around 100,000 SHU. Originating from North East Mexico where pods are often pickled or used in salsas and sauces. Pequin chilli plants are generally quite compact, growing up to 60cm tall in the first season but they can grow taller if successfully overwintered.
